Our Services

Re-Upholstery & Restoration

Give your cherished furniture a new lease of life. We expertly re-upholster sofas, chairs, and all types of furniture, using premium fabrics and materials. From minor repairs to complete restorations, we’ll make your old favourites look and feel brand new.

Bespoke Furniture

Looking for something unique? We design and craft bespoke furniture tailored to your style and space. From concept to creation, our team brings your vision to life with quality craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Vintage Furniture Revival

Restore the charm of your vintage and antique pieces. Our specialists carefully repair, refinish, and re-upholster vintage furniture, preserving its character while ensuring comfort and durability for years to come.

Premium Fabrics & Materials
