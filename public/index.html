About CK Upholstery

CK Upholstery is a family-run business built on decades of experience, passion, and craftsmanship.

Our story begins with Murat Yilmaz, who started his journey in upholstery at just 14 years old, working on boats and yachts along the Turkish coast. Driven by a love for the craft, Murat moved to the UK and, together with Gemma, founded CK Upholstery.

What began in the back of a garage in Waltham Cross quickly grew through dedication and word of mouth. After completing hundreds of bespoke projects for local clients, CK Upholstery expanded to a full workshop and showroom in Epping, Essex, where our talented team now serves homes and businesses across the region.

Today, we’re proud to offer expert upholstery, restoration, and custom furniture services—always with a personal touch and a commitment to quality.
